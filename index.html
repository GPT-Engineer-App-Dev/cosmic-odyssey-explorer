About Space

Space, also known as the cosmos, is the vast expanse that exists beyond Earth and between celestial bodies. It's characterized by its near-perfect vacuum, containing low densities of particles, predominantly hydrogen and helium, as well as electromagnetic radiation, magnetic fields, neutrinos, cosmic rays, and dark matter.
